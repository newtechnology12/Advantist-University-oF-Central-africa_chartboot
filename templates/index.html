We Are Professional in IT

This responds to the needs of Rwandans and the Government of Rwanda whose desire is to engage in ICT. The Adventist University of Central Africa supports the Government of Rwanda in this endeavour by producing teachers capable of helping in the secondary schools.,
The degree of Bachelor of Education with Information Technology is intended to help AUCA future teachers to be able to teach information technology or computer in the secondary schools.

Have the Courage to have Convictions

The Adventist University of Central Africa operates on the basis of the Seventh-day Adventist worldview, which holds that God is the Creator and Sustainer of the universe and the source of true knowledge. The entrance of sin caused man’s alienation from God, therefore the restoration of the relation between man and his God is the main aim of the Christian Education that leads students to discover and understand the truth through critical thinking.
